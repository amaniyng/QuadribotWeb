Smart Solution for FOG (Fat, Oil, and Grease) Interceptors

QuadriBot is proud to have developed this innovative solution for managing FOG (Fat, Oil, and Grease) interceptors for one the international leaders of the Water Industry companies. From the initial specification phase to full industrialization, we have been deeply involved in every step of this project to ensure a robust and effective outcome.

Key Features

Data Transmission Gateway

We developed a gateway that transmits sensor data to the cloud, ensuring real-time monitoring and management.

Mobile Application

Our user-friendly mobile app allows direct communication with the gateway and/or the cloud, providing users with up-to-date information on grease levels.

Enhanced Security

Our solution prioritizes security, protecting data and ensuring safe operations.

Over-the-Air Updates (OTA)

We support OTA updates, enabling seamless upgrades and maintenance without the need for physical access.

Our bespoke solution exemplifies our commitment to innovation and excellence in the field of automotive technology.
